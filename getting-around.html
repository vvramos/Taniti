Airport

Taniti International Airport

Address: 100 Airstrip Rd, Taniti City, Taniti

Hours: Open daily, 5:00 AM - 11:00 PM

Phone: (555) 123-4567

Info: Taniti International Airport serves small jets and propeller planes. Expansion plans are underway to accommodate larger jets. The airport offers a small lounge, a café, and free Wi-Fi. Rental car services and taxis are available just outside the terminal.

Cruise Ship

Yellow Leaf Bay Cruise Dock

Address: 200 Portside Ln, Merriton Landing, Taniti

Hours: Wednesdays, 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM

Phone: (555) 234-5678

Info: A small cruise ship arrives once a week, allowing passengers to explore Taniti for a full day. Visitors can find taxis and bike rentals near the dock for easy transportation.

Public Buses

Taniti City Bus Terminal

Address: 50 Transit Ave, Taniti City, Taniti

Taniti City Route: 5:00 AM - 11:00 PM (Every 15 minutes)

Island-Wide Routes: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM (Every 30 minutes)

Fare: $2 per ride, $10 for a day pass

Phone: (555) 345-6789

Info: Public buses cover Taniti City and major tourist areas. Private buses connect to remote parts of the island. Exact fare required; cash and major cards accepted.

Taxis

Taniti Taxi Services

Address: 15 Harbor St, Taniti City, Taniti

Availability: 24/7

Phone: (555) 456-7890

Info: Taxis are available in Taniti City and at major tourist spots. Standard fare starts at $5, with an additional $2 per mile. Rides can be booked via phone or hailed on the street.

Bike Rentals

Taniti Bikes

Address: 80 Seaside Blvd, Merriton Landing, Taniti

Hours: 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Rates: $10/hour, $30/day (Helmet included)

Phone: (555) 567-8901

Info: Explore the island on two wheels! Helmets are required by law. Taniti City and Merriton Landing are bike-friendly, with scenic paths along the coast.

Rental Cars

Taniti Island Car Rentals

Address: 5 Airport Rd, Taniti City, Taniti

Hours: 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Rates: Starting at $50/day

Phone: (555) 678-9012

Info: A variety of rental cars are available near the airport. Drivers must be 21+ with a valid license. Reservations are recommended during peak season.

Hotel Shuttle

Taniti Resort Shuttle Service

Address: Various hotel locations

Hours: 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM (Every 30 minutes)

Phone: (555) 789-0123

Info: Many hotels provide complimentary shuttle services between the airport, downtown, and key attractions. Check with your hotel for specific routes and schedules.
